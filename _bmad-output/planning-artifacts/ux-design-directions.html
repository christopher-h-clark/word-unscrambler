Evaluation Guide: How to Choose

✅ Layout Intuitiveness

Which information hierarchy matches your priorities? Does the flow feel natural and obvious?

✅ Interaction Style

Which interaction patterns match your core experience? Do buttons and inputs feel responsive and clear?

✅ Visual Weight

Which visual density feels right? Does it feel spacious (clean and calm) or efficient (compact)?

✅ Results Presentation

How should results be displayed? Single column, cards, grid, or flowing text?

✅ Button Prominence

Should the button be a subtle accent or a bold focal point? How obvious should it be?

✅ Brand Alignment

Which direction best supports your emotional goals? Does it feel professional, minimal, and calm?
